CS | STRENGTH

Measuring and performing static training just got smarter

CS / STRENGTH aims to help personal trainers, physiotherapists and
sports scientists to spot muscle injuries and rehabilitate faster.
Connected Senses will be powered by our open platform and app.

Why strength

People love training data and hence wearables have become a billion dollar market. But, a sad 45% of wearables end up in kitchen drawers after just few weeks of usage. This puts a negative impact. Strength is not a wearable, but a tool to get instant data from the muscles and train along in a static fashion.Static training helps improve static strength as several research shows.This in turn helps people perform better in their dynamic training sessions

Effective Training

Starting from pointing out weak spots in muscle groups, the analysis helps both trainers and the user. They understand what the muscles challenges are before finding a suitable training routine. No more wrong routines, when one can spot it easily.

Accessibility

Imagine having Strength in your favourite fitness clubs. All you have to do is connect your smartphone and select the muscle groups to train on your app. Strength can accommodate different muscle harnesses and this gives the user to choose variety of muscle groups
